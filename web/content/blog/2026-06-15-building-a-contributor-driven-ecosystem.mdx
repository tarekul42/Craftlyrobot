Craftly is not a traditional startup. We are a contributor-driven ecosystem.

## Why Contributor-Driven?

The traditional startup model is centralized: a founder raises money, hires a team, builds a product, and sells it. We wanted something different — where anyone can contribute based on passion, not paychecks.

## Our Approach

1. **Anyone can apply** — no resume gatekeeping
2. **You choose your commitment** — 5h, 10h, 20h, or full-time
3. **We mentor you** — structured 30-day onboarding with a real task
4. **You own your work** — contributors are listed as authors

## The 30-Day Onboarding Ramp

After applying and being accepted, every new contributor goes through:
- **Week 1**: Setup, meet your mentor, learn the stack
- **Week 2**: First small task (documentation, bug fix)
- **Week 3**: Medium task with mentor review
- **Week 4**: Ship your first significant contribution

If this sounds like something you want to be part of, apply today.
