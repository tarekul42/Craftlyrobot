# Hello World from Bangladesh

Every tech ecosystem has an origin story. Ours starts in Dhaka.

Bangladesh has tens of thousands of talented engineers, designers, and builders. Most of them end up working for companies elsewhere. The talent leaves; the value leaves with it. We're trying to change that.

## Why we're building Craftly

We're not trying to build another startup. We're trying to build Bangladesh's first real tech company — one that competes with Google, Microsoft, and Apple. Not by copying them, but by building something they can't: a contributor-driven ecosystem where the work happens in the open, and the people doing the work own the outcome.

That sounds ambitious. It is. But ambition is cheap. What matters is execution.

<Callout type="info" title="What we're building first">
  Craftly Robot — software that builds Android apps pretty fast. You connect your Android phone via USB, tell our robot what to build, and done.
</Callout>

## The foundation

We didn't start from scratch. Three products taught us what we know:

1. **Hello** — our onboarding robot, learning language nuances from real conversations with real people
2. **Workspace** — already integrated with our decentralized networking layer, getting battle-tested daily
3. **Agent Console** — our internal DevOps tool, running real-world use cases every day

These three are the foundation. They generate the proprietary knowledge that powers Craftly Robot.

## Why Bangladesh

A lot of people ask: why not move to Silicon Valley?

The answer is simple. The talent is here. The cost of living is lower, which means we can do more with less. And the market — 170 million people in Bangladesh, billions more across South Asia — is underserved by global tech.

We're not trying to leave. We're trying to put Bangladesh on the map.

<Quote author="Wasif" role="Founder">
  We're not trying to build another startup. We're trying to build Bangladesh's first real tech company.
</Quote>

## What's next

Craftly Robot is in active development. We're opening up early access soon. If you want to be part of this — as a contributor, an early user, or a supporter — [we want to hear from you](/contribute/apply).

This is just the beginning. Welcome to Craftly.
