After months of incremental reliability work, Workspace crossed the 99% uptime mark for the first time.

## The Journey

**Three months ago**: 94% uptime. Nodes would go offline unpredictably. Sync conflicts were common.
**One month ago**: 97% uptime. Better node health monitoring, automated reconnection.
**Today**: 99.2% uptime. Still not 99.9% (our target), but we're close.

## What Made the Difference

1. **Node health checks** — every node pings a health endpoint every 30 seconds
2. **Automatic reconnection** — if a node drops, it reconnects within 5 seconds
3. **Conflict resolution** — CRDT-based merging handles concurrent edits
4. **Monitoring** — real-time dashboards for the ops team

## What's Next

Our next target is 99.9% uptime with geographic distribution and better alerting. If you have experience with distributed systems reliability, we'd love your help on this.
