Hello, our onboarding robot, just got a major upgrade. It can now understand and respond to conversations that mix Bengali, English, and Hinglish seamlessly.

## Why This Matters

Bangladesh is a multilingual society. Most people code-switch between Bangla and English multiple times in a single sentence. A robot that only understands English would feel foreign and frustrating.

## The Technical Challenge

Code-switching is hard for language models because grammar shifts mid-sentence, scripts get mixed, and context matters. We approached this by training on real conversation data from our community channels.

## What Changed in v2

- Intent recognition across all three language variants
- Response generation that matches the user's language mix
- Fallback handling for unrecognized inputs

Early results: 92% intent recognition accuracy across mixed-language inputs.

## Next Steps

We're working on expanding to more regional languages. If you speak Sylheti, Chittagonian, or other regional dialects, we'd love your help.
